ABOUT US

We located in Nairobi, Moi Avenue street Mugaini House opposite Hilton hotel. We offer pizza of high quality which has white and red oak for its clean taste, high BTUs (British thermal units) and mild ash bed. All of our pizzas, wings, appetizers, and entrees are cooked to perfection at a consistent 800 degrees.
The blaze of the oven leaves its unique mark on all of our foods with a signature charred bubble on the crust of our pizzas, delivery is offered and charged according to your location.
